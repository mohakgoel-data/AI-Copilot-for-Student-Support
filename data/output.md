# Statement of Purpose: Reinforce AI/ML Club Selection Process
### Scaler School of Technology
August 19, 2025

## 1 Introduction

The Reinforce AI/ML Club at Scaler School of Technology aims to foster a community of passionate learners and innovators in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The selection process for the club is designed to identify enthusiastic and capable students through a structured contest and, for intermediate candidates, an additional interview stage. This document outlines the selection process, syllabus, and schedule for the Reinforce AI/ML Club entry.

## 2 Selection Process

The selection process consists of a single AI/ML Entry Contest with two sections: Beginner and Intermediate. Candidates will participate in the contest based on their self-assessed proficiency level. The contest is designed to evaluate candidates’ understanding of fundamental and advanced concepts in programming and machine learning.

### 2.1 Contest Details

* **Date and Time:** 25 August 2025, 5:45 PM – 7:30 PM (90 minutes)
* **Venue:** Class C, 2nd Floor
* **Format:** The contest comprises two sections in a single test:
    - Beginner Section: Questions covering basic Python programming concepts.
    - Intermediate Section: Questions covering Python programming, NumPy, TensorFlow, and an overview of Machine Learning.
* **Requirements:**
    - A fully charged laptop with a stable Wi-Fi connection.
    - All submissions must be made through the designated contest platform.
* **Rules:**
    - Each question carries specific marks as indicated in the contest.
    - Submissions must be completed within the 90-minute duration.
    - Incomplete or late submissions will not be considered.

## 2.2 Interview Stage (Intermediate Candidates)

* Candidates who perform well in the Intermediate section of the contest will be invited for an interview.
* The interview will assess deeper technical knowledge, problem-solving skills, and passion for AI/ML.
* Details of the interview schedule will be communicated to shortlisted candidates after the contest.

# 3 Syllabus

The contest syllabus is divided into two categories based on the sections.

## 3.1 Beginner Section

The Beginner section focuses on foundational Python programming concepts, including:

* How Python code is executed
* Variables
* Receiving input
* Type conversion
* Strings
* Arithmetic operations
* If statements
* Logical operators
* Comparison operators
* While loops
* For loops
* Nested loops
* 1D lists
* Tuples
* Functions

## 3.2 Intermediate Section

The Intermediate section includes all topics from the Beginner section, plus the following:

* Python syntax (as covered in the Beginner section)
* NumPy

* TensorFlow
* Machine Learning overview

# 5 Conclusion

The Reinforce AI/ML Club at Scaler School of Technology welcomes students who demonstrate enthusiasm and competence in AI and ML. The selection process, through a well-structured contest and interview stage, ensures that motivated and skilled individuals join the club to contribute to its goals of learning and innovation. Candidates are advised to prepare thoroughly, ensure their laptops are charged, and adhere to the contest rules for a fair evaluation.
